Kobolds Design Doc

-- Feel --

Frantic, panicky fast-paced gameplay
Silly light atmosphere, cartoony violence

-- Gameplay --

	Stealing
Steal items and hide them in a stash
	Running Away
Bad guys should be able to kill you most of the time
	Traps
Feeds into running away, you have to outwit the enemies so you don't die
	Dying
A necessary setback, respawning should be quick, and without a super hard punishment
